HTML-Overview

  • Text content
  • Markup
  • References to documents (links)
  • Markup - made up of tags

HTML-Elements

We covered bold (b), italics (em), Links (a), images (img), line breaks (br), and paragraphs initially.

Inline and block elements were also covered. Examples of incline elemants are "br", "span", and "img". Examples of block elements (for which you are able to customize height, width, etc) are "p", "form", and "div".
